Reforestation (Carbon offset) - €80K
The entire land was fully burned down in 2017, destroying a big part of the native trees. Now whats is growing back is basically 1 species, mimosas. A non native invasive tree that grows fast and takes the sunlight for other plants. This makes it in the long term very hard to create a healthy diverse forrest life and will have much higher changes for wild fires again in the future. Forrest fires are a big challenge in Portugal but we can help to prevent them. We will work with ecologists to analyse the land, create a landscape plan, clean the current land and start planting a strong forrest. For years to come.
